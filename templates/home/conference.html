Trends by keyword

It's interesting to see topics such as cloud, graph, tool, mapreduce, social, and online coming into the scene in the past few years.

Also, it seems that technology and xml, are taking a downturn, as is relational -- is this the turning point for NoSQL? Strange to see OLAP slowing down, given the popularity of large data these days.
